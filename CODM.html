CALL OF DUTY: MOBILE

Call of Duty Mobile provides users with basic stats of their last 50 games.

Terms and Definitions

>
Term Definition
Normal Kills Kills you get through your primary or secondary weapon
Assists When 2 or more players shoot the same enemy, the player with the most hits gets the normal score while the other player(s) get an assist score
Scorestreaks Kills you get from using your scorestreak weapon. They are different from primary/secondary weapons. You need to acquire an assigned amount of points to use scorestreak weapons
Operator Skills Kills you get from using your operator weapon. They are different from primary/secondary weapons. The weapon is available to use after an X amount of time
Destroyed Items Score you get from destroying enemy grenades/trip mines. When you destroy an enemy scorestreak weapon, it gets counted here.
Captured Objectives Available in Domination Mode. Points you get for capturing the objective
Capture Kills Enemies you kill who were in the objective
Others I honestly have no idea
